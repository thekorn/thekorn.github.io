# 2026 - the year of CSS

A new year, a new challenge - in 2026 i will try to improove my css skills. And whats better to do so by implementing different idea, mockups and various other things.
There is only one constraint: lets do as much as possible using tailwind. WTF, why tailwind you might say... Thats relativly easy: I am a strong believer of implementing your design systems using utility classes.
There is more: I am not a frontend expert, in fact, i am coming from the backend world. And for me tailwind is way more easy and readable than other CSS conventions.
And i would like to proof to myself that im right: tailwind is easy, and there is nothing you cannot do with it.

And whats better to get familiar with tech - whats better to get a feel of how stuff is working than just using the stuff you want to learn! - thats why i will implement new stuff every week.
